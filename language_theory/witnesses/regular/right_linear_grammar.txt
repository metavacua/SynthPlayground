# Corrected Right-Linear Grammar for the language a^n b^m where n, m > 0
# e.g., ab, aab, abbb
# Productions are of the form A -> tB or A -> t.

# S: Start symbol
# A: Generates the rest of the a's (a*) and transitions to the b's (b+)
# B: Generates the rest of the b's (b*)

S -> aA
A -> aA | bB
B -> bB | b
